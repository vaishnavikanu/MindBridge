WHO MiNDbank: More Inclusiveness Needed in Disability and Development
The MiNDbank is an online platform, managed by the Centre for Mental Health Law & Policy, bringing together country and international resources, covering mental health, substance abuse, disability, general health, human rights and development. It is part of WHO’s QualityRights campaign to end violations against people with mental disabilities. MiNDbank aims to facilitate dialogue, advocacy and research, to promote reform in these areas in line with international human rights and best practice standards.
